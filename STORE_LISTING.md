## Full Description

My Favorite Book helps children write, illustrate, and share their own stories. With a friendly step-by-step wizard, kids choose characters, settings, and time periods, then bring their stories to life with AI-generated illustrations.

DESIGNED FOR EVERY LEARNER
Built with Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles, the app includes features specifically designed for children with ADHD, dyslexia, and other learning differences:
- Sentence starters and word banks to overcome writer's block
- Visual story progress map to maintain focus
- Gentle idle nudges that encourage without pressure
- Effort-based badges and rewards for motivation
- OpenDyslexic font option and focus mode
- Voice input and text-to-speech read aloud

KEY FEATURES
- Step-by-step story creation wizard
- AI Story Buddy writing assistant
- AI-generated page illustrations and book covers
- Customizable avatar with AI portrait generation
- Read aloud with word highlighting
- Voice-to-text story dictation
- PDF export and print support
- Classroom mode for teachers and students
- Coin economy with effort-based rewards
- Dark galaxy theme designed for comfortable reading

FOR TEACHERS
Create classrooms, share codes with students, collect story submissions, and track creative writing progress — all from a dedicated teacher dashboard. 14-day free trial included.

SAFE FOR KIDS
- COPPA compliant
- Parental verification gate before any purchases
- No ads, no tracking, no data sold to third parties
- Works offline for story writing (AI features require internet)

Start with a free account — upgrade to Family or Teacher plans for unlimited books, illustrations, and features.
